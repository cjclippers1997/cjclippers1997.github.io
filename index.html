The Barber

A Half-Century of Craft

Jack McCormick isn't just a barber — he's a craftsman with five decades of experience behind the chair. He started cutting hair in Rhode Island in the mid-1970s, honing his skills for 20 years before bringing his expertise north to Nashua, New Hampshire.

In 1997, Jack opened CJ Clipper Barber Shop at 15 Spring Street, and it's been a neighborhood staple ever since. No gimmicks. No apps. Just a skilled barber, a good conversation, and the kind of haircut you keep coming back for.
